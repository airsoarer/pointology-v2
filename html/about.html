What is Pointology?

What it is

Pointology is a non-bracket multiplayer game where you compete against league members to pick which teams from the Division-1 Men’s Basketball Championship will score the most points. The concept is simple:

  • Pick 16 teams—1 team per seed
  • Total the points scored from your teams (win or lose) over seven rounds
  • The player who picks the teams with the highest total points scored wins!

What it isn't

Pointology is not a bracket-based game. Instead, we focus on the points your teams score. The reason I invented this game is because too many newbies joining the Division-1 tournament were intimidated by the size and scope of the Tournment bracket—68 teams presented too many choices! By simply selecting 16 teams, his version of the tournament is now transformed into a more fan friendly game.

About the Designer

TONY YONTO , an avid sports enthusiast, developed Pointology in 2002. Tweaking his novel game for almost two decades, has engineered an innovative approach to following the mens college basketball tournament. The spark came when he thought ... who needs a bracket ? Freed from the constraints of endless paper brackets, Tony transformed the way his family & friends spend their weekends in March.
